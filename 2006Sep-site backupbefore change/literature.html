Mile High Area Literature Committee
 
You can expedite your groups literature order by completing the online order form and emailing it to the literature committee by 10 am the Sunday of the  area meeting.

Please Read The instructions Carfuly.
1.Down load the order form  
2. Note this is an excel form with imbedded macros you should receive a warning message warning you about macros including viruses please do not disable macros this will prevent the form from auto calculating. This file is viris free
3. Fill out the order form by identifying the number of items the you would like to order. Do not calculate totals
4. Fill in the Group information at the top.
5. Press the calculate totals button located at the bottom of the sheet.
6. Select save as located in the file menu.
7. Save the file with your group name and the date.
9. Email the file to The literature Committee 
10. You will be able to pickup your literature at the next MHASC meeting please bring payment personal Checks and Money orders
