Professionals Requesting a Narcotics Anonymous Hospitals & Institutions Meeting At Your Facility:
" Please complete the H & I Meeting Request Form and email to nacolorado.org.
" The H & I Chair Marilou L. - 720-318-4924 will contact the prospective facility explaining what NA has to offer and answer any questions about the process for adopting a new meeting.
" Then the H & I Chair will submit facility request to the next subcommittee meeting held on the second Sunday of the month at 6750 Carr in Arvada. To expedite the process, we encourage a representative from the facility (requesting a meeting) to be our guest at the next H & I Subcommittee.
" Committee approval and setting the start date is contingent upon having an adequate number of volunteers from our fellowship step up to conduct new H & I Meeting at your facility.
" We are unable to establish meeting at any facility that requires us to pay fees.
" We welcome you to view a List of Facilities that H & I currently serves.
     Before A Meeting Is Approved:
" The subcommittee elects a panel coordinator as a liaison between the subcommittee and the new facility.
" The panel coordinator solicits enough volunteers to facilitate the proposed meeting.
" Then the panel coordinator reports progress at the next subcommittee meeting wherein a start date is established or not.
    Once A Meeting Is Approved:
" The panel coordinator and the facility contact establish a meeting schedule.
" The panel coordinator submits literature needs to the H & I subcommittee for approval and inclusion in our budget.
" The H & I secretary provides the panel coordinator with a start up folder containing meeting formats, a set of readings, panel coordinator report forms.

Members Of The Narcotics Anonymous Fellowship Interested In Getting Involved With Hospitals & Institutions Service:
" We welcome you to view a List of Facilities that H & I currently serves. It provides information with regards to locations, meeting dates and times, and clean time requirements. All helpful information in narrowing down what facilities you are interested in.
" Then we request that you complete a New Volunteer Form and email it to nacolorado.org.
" There are two requirements to become an H & I Volunteers: attend an orientation/training seminar held quarterly and attend an H & I meeting as a guest at one of numerous facilities we serve. All that we ask is that you make arrangements with the respective panel coordinator of that facility (see List of Facilities).
" It is highly recommended, but not a requirement, to attend an H & I Subcommittee meeting held on the second Sunday of the month at 6750 Carr in Arvada.
" If you have any questions please feel free to contact the H & I Chair Marilou L at 720-318-4924.
